About

Unique Indian Handicrafts by Aritisans

The Bidri art form is an elegant metal handicraft that is created using intricate inlay work of silver and gold, on an alloy of zinc and copper, where the zinc gives it a rich black colour.

Bidri craft gets its name from Bidar, located in Karnataka. The artisans create unique designs consisting of flowers, leaves, symmetrical geometric patterns, stylized plants, and figures, Bidri ware is used to make different products like Hookahs, vases, plates, and paanholders.The Salar Jung museum possesses many household Bidri items. The intricate metal artwork is a reflection of royalty. The shining silver motifs inlay on the lustrous black metal are a prized possession for any craft enthusiast.
